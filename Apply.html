Three Main Tracks

You don’t have to lock yourself into a single lane forever. Many recruits start in one track and shift as they gain confidence and experience.

  • Licensed reps leverage experience and licenses for higher-level deals.
  • D2D pros transition from other verticals into higher-paying solar programs.
  • Deal submitters monetize relationships and referrals with low time commitment.
What Happens After You Apply

Once your application comes in, Salvador or someone on the leadership side will:

  • Review your background and track selection.
  • Reach out by WhatsApp, phone, or email if it’s a fit.
  • Place you into the correct onboarding path and training modules.
  • Help you focus on clear first objectives within your first 30–90 days.
